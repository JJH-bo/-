# V6.3.2 Acceptance Notes

Current stage: Manhattan island public validation build.

Scope:
- Manhattan main island is the core city.
- Liberty Island and Battery Park form the concept entrance.
- SoHo and Greenwich form the first order equation district.
- Chelsea and High Line form the reducible equation industrial district.
- Midtown and Times Square form the higher order linear core.
- Central Park is the only large open space and carries geometry applications.
- East River piers carry physics applications.
- Governors Island carries risk training.
- Grey Island remains a distant optional difference equation area.

Completed:
1. City data has been rebuilt around Manhattan island geography.
2. CityBuilder now assembles water, land masses, district surfaces, plazas, streets, urban fabric, bridges, landmarks and metro lines.
3. BuildingFactory now has semantic building types instead of one box type.
4. CityDetailLayer adds Times Square screens, High Line, Central Park details, East River piers, Liberty ferry and Governors Island risk facilities.
5. LabelManager now limits visible labels and hides near labels to avoid text clutter.
6. PanelManager now uses V6.3 Manhattan semantics.
7. Public preview has been synced to V6.3.2.
8. City data validation has been tightened for V6.3 required districts, islands and landmarks.

Acceptance focus:
- Far view should read as a city.
- Mid view should distinguish major districts.
- Near view should show meaningful landmarks and click panels.
- Roads, water, parks, bridges and piers should carry learning meaning.
- Labels should not dominate the view.
